Details About Nisha Hospital

At Nisha Hospital, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality healthcare services to our patients and their families. Our mission is to deliver exceptional medical care with a personal touch, ensuring that every individual who walks through our doors feels valued, respected, and cared for.

Our Services

Emergency Services

Emergency Care When You Need It Most

Our emergency department is equipped to handle all types of emergencies, from minor injuries to life-threatening conditions. Our team of experienced doctors and nurses are available 24/7 to provide immediate care.

Diagnostic Services

Accurate Diagnostics for Better Treatment

Our state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and expert radiologists ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatment plans. From X-rays to MRIs, we offer a range of diagnostic services.

Surgical Services

Expert Surgical Care for a Faster Recovery

Our experienced surgeons and anesthesiologists provide top-notch surgical care, from routine procedures to complex operations. We prioritize patient safety and comfort.

Maternity Services

Welcoming Your New Arrival with Care

Our maternity services provide a supportive and nurturing environment for new mothers and their families. From prenatal care to postpartum support, we're here for you.

Pediatric Services

Caring for Your Little Ones

Our pediatricians and pediatric specialists provide comprehensive care for children, from routine check-ups to treatment of acute illnesses.

Rehabilitation Services

Helping You Recover and Thrive

Our rehabilitation services, including physical, occupational, and speech therapy, help patients recover from injuries, illnesses, or surgeries.

Laboratory Services

Accurate Lab Results for Better Health

Our laboratory services provide accurate and timely test results, helping your healthcare provider diagnose and treat medical conditions.

Pharmacy Services

Convenient Pharmacy Services

Our on-site pharmacy offers convenient access to medications, expert advice from pharmacists, and competitive pricing.

Health Education Services

Empowering You with Health Knowledge

Our health education programs and resources help patients and families make informed decisions about their health and wellness.
